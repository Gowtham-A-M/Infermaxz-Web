Python for AI & ML — learning roadmap

High-level journey aligned with our official course brochure. Your facilitator may fine-tune pacing and depth per cohort.

  1. 1

    Python foundations for AI

    Syntax, data structures, functions, OOP, virtual environments, and Git—so you can work confidently in real codebases.

  2. 2

    Data stack & wrangling

    NumPy and Pandas for numerical computing, cleaning, joins, and feature prep—the daily toolkit for ML datasets.

  3. 3

    Visualization & EDA

    Matplotlib and Seaborn for exploratory analysis, communicating insights, and validating assumptions before you model.

  4. 4

    Classical machine learning

    scikit-learn: train/test splits, metrics, pipelines, regression, classification, clustering, and model selection.

  5. 5

    Deep learning core

    Neural network concepts, training loops, and frameworks—TensorFlow / Keras and PyTorch foundations for tabular, image, and sequence models.

  6. 6

    Applied AI workflows

    Practical NLP and computer vision patterns, plus how pretrained models and modern tooling accelerate delivery.

  7. 7

    Capstone & next steps

    End-to-end project, model packaging, simple deployment or API exposure, and portfolio prep—mirroring the brochure’s outcomes section.

FAQ

Who is this program for?

Learners from about grades 5 through 12. Cohorts may be grouped by age band so activities stay appropriate.

Do students need coding experience?

No. The emphasis is on no-code and tool-based creation, with clear guardrails and mentor support.

Is it safe for children to use AI tools?

We stress responsible use, supervised sessions, age-appropriate prompts, and privacy hygiene. Parents receive a short orientation on what to expect.

What if my child misses a live session?

Recordings are provided for revision where available, and mentors summarize key takeaways for the cohort.

Will there be a certificate?

Yes—students who complete the program requirements receive an Infermaxz Systems certificate suitable for portfolios.
